A little about Pat's Cookies.


Hello cookie lovers! My name is Pat and we are the Chinhooks family. I come from a family of fishers from a small town on a very small island off of a very small country in the southern part of the world. I am the baker, the cookie magic maker. People go nuts for our cookies so my partner Peter is the store's security. My son Parker is my baking assistant, he too has the magic cookie touch and my daughter Penelope is the cookie and merch designer.

If you love Salmon and have a deep appreciation for scaley beauties or identify salmon as your spirit animal than you are at the right place.
